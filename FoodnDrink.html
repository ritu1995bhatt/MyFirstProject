MY FAVOURITE FOOD

Though I am not a Foodie!!!

But I love to eat Indian food which is One of the best international cuisines. Indian cuisine has travelled all across the globe. It is not that hard to find a good Indian restaurant near your location. Indian cuisine encompasses all dishes that are native to India. Now, it can be pretty mystifying why even non-Indians love Indian cuisine. Well, here are some good reasons on why people love Indian food.

because of the spices.A lot of people love Indian cuisine because it is generally healthy. Most Indian people are very conscious about their health. Some practice healthy eating because of religious reasons while some really just do themselves a favor. With the traditional cooking practices and very healthy ingredients used to make Indian dishes, it is not a surprise why Indians are generally healthier than most national groups in Europe. It is also seasoned with various herbs and spices that help create its distinct taste.
